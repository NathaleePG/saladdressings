3 Simple Salad Dressing Recipes

June 28th, 2018

  • Green Goddess Salad Dressing

    1-2 Servings
    VEGAN

    Ingredients:

    5 tbsp vegan mayonnaise
    1/2 of a lime
    1 garlic clove
    1 green onion stalk
    1 tsp dried parsley
    1 tsp dried basil
    1/2 tsp onion powder
    pinch of salt & pepper
    3 tbsp olive oil
    2 tbsp water (use less if you want a thicker dressing)

    What you need:

    Small-medium sized bowl
    Fork
    Measuring spoons
    Grater
    Small whisk (optional)
    Lemon squeezer (optional)

      Instructions:

    1. Measure out the vegan mayonnaise in a small-medium sized bowl
    2. Cut half of a lime and use a squeezer or a fork to squeeze out all the juice into the bowl
    3. Peel a garlic clove and grate it on the grater. Get as much as you can but be careful with your fingers!
    4. Take the onion stalk and cut off the thick white part. Use the white part to grate into the bowl (do the best you can). Then take the green leafy part of the onion and chop it coarsely. You can chop it as fine or as coarse as you like, it’s all preference!
    5. Add in all of the dried herbs and spices, salt and pepper.
    6. Add in the olive oil and water then mix with a small whisk or a fork.
    7. Put the dressing in a small mason jar or a small tupperware and store it in the fridge to keep it fresh. It should last about 3 days. Make sure to shake the mason jar before eating after it has been stored in the fridge. Enjoy!

  • Asian Peanut Ginger Dressing

    1-2 Servings
    VEGAN

    Ingredients:

    5 tbsp vegan mayonnaise
    3 tbsp vegan mayonnaise
    2 tbsp all natural or organic peanut butter
    1/2 of a lime
    1 tsp apple cider vinegar
    1 garlic clove
    1 tsp grated ginger
    1/2 tsp onion powder
    1/2 tsp paprika
    pinch of salt & pepper

    3 tbsp olive oil

    2 tbsp water (use less if you want a thicker dressing)

    What you need:

    Small-medium sized bowl
    Fork
    Measuring spoons
    Grater
    Small whisk (optional)
    Lemon squeezer (optional)

      Instructions:

    1. Measure out the vegan mayonnaise in a small-medium sized bowl
    2. Cut half of a lime and use a squeezer or a fork to squeeze out all the juice into the bowl
    3. Peel a garlic clove and grate it on the grater. Get as much as you can but be careful with your fingers!
    4. Grate 1 thumb of organic ginger and measure out 1 tsp. If you don’t have organic ginger, make sure to peel off the skin.
    5. Add in all of the dried herbs and spices, salt and pepper.
    6. Add in the olive oil and water then mix with a small whisk or a fork.
    7. Put the dressing in a small mason jar or a small tupperware and store it in the fridge to keep it fresh. It should last about 3 days. Make sure to shake the mason jar before eating after it has been stored in the fridge. Enjoy!

  • Garlic Balsamic Dressing

    1-2 Servings
    VEGAN

    Ingredients:

    5 tbsp vegan mayonnaise
    Garlic Balsamic Dressing
    5 tbsp balsamic vinegar
    1 clove of garlic
    1/2 tsp paprika
    pinch of salt & pepper
    1 tsp red pepper flakes (optional if you want it spicy)

    What you need:

    Small-medium sized bowl
    Fork
    Measuring spoons
    Grater
    Small whisk (optional)

      Instructions:

    1. Measure out the olive oil and balsamic vinegar into a bowl.
    2. Peel a garlic clove and grate it on the grater. Get as much as you can but be careful with your fingers!
    3. Add in all of the dried spices, salt and pepper.
    4. If you want a spicy kick to your salad, add in some red pepper flakes! If you don’t like it spicy, just leave it out.
    5. Mix with a small whisk or a fork.
    6. Put the dressing in a small mason jar or a small tupperware and store it in the fridge to keep it fresh. It should last about 3 days. Make sure to shake the mason jar before eating and after it has been stored in the fridge. Enjoy!
